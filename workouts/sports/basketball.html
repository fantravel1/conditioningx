Basketball Fitness Demands

Basketball is a game of repeated short bursts‚Äîsprinting the floor, jumping for rebounds, defending in stance. You need explosive power that you can access again and again, plus the aerobic base to recover between plays. The fourth quarter separates good players from great ones.

Jump Training (Plyometrics)

Repeated vertical power is what separates ballers.

Box Jump Series

  • Box jumps: 3x8
  • Depth jumps: 3x5
  • Single leg hops: 3x6 each
  • Full recovery between sets

Rebound Jumps

  • Continuous jumps: 3x10
  • Minimal ground contact
  • Arms drive up each time
  • Like grabbing boards

Approach Jumps

  • 1-step approach: 3x6
  • 2-step approach: 3x6
  • Off both feet
  • Game-like takeoffs

Defensive Conditioning

Defensive Slides Circuit

40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest. 4 rounds:

  • Station 1: Lateral slides (sideline to sideline)
  • Station 2: Closeouts (sprint to hand up, backpedal)
  • Station 3: Zig-zag slides (5 slides each direction)
  • Station 4: Drop step + sprint (react and chase)

Stay low. Hands active. This is where games are won.
