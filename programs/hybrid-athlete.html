Progression Over 8 Weeks

Strength

Add weight when you complete all prescribed reps with good form. Aim for 2.5-5 lb increases on upper body, 5-10 lb on lower body.

Conditioning

Week 1-4: Build duration. Week 5-8: Add intensity to intervals while maintaining Zone 2 volume.
