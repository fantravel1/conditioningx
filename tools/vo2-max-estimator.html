What is VO2 Max?

VO2 max is the maximum rate at which your body can use oxygen during intense exercise. It's considered the gold standard measure of cardiovascular fitness.

Higher VO2 max = better endurance performance and cardiovascular health.

Improving Your VO2 Max

Zone 2 Training

Build your aerobic base with 80% of training at low intensity (conversational pace). This builds mitochondria and capillary density.

High-Intensity Intervals

Include 1-2 sessions per week of intervals at 90-100% max HR. 4x4 minute intervals with 3-4 min recovery is highly effective.

Consistency

VO2 max improves 5-20% over 8-12 weeks with consistent training. Beginners see the largest gains.
