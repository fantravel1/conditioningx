Role at ConditioningX

Hypertrophy + Cardio Integration

Dr. Mike addresses one of the most common concerns: "Will cardio kill my gains?" His research and practical experience show how strength athletes can incorporate conditioning without sacrificing muscle growth.

Video Content Model

With millions of YouTube subscribers, Dr. Mike has cracked the code on making exercise science content engaging and accessible. His format inspires our approach to educational content.

Why He's Essential

Dr. Israetel reaches an audience that often views cardio with suspicion—the strength and bodybuilding community. His endorsement of conditioning as compatible with (and beneficial for) muscle building helps break down barriers between training modalities.

Key Insight from Dr. Mike

"Cardio doesn't kill gains. Being out of shape, having poor recovery, and avoiding all conditioning—that's what limits your potential. Smart cardio makes you a better athlete."
