The Travel Challenge

Travel disrupts routines. Jet lag, irregular schedules, limited space, no equipment. This program provides flexible workouts you can do anywhere—hotel room, airport, Airbnb—to maintain your conditioning while traveling.

Workout Library

Mix and match based on your time, energy, and space. All workouts are bodyweight only.

Quick Workouts (10-15 min)

The Wake-Up

Perfect for jet lag or early mornings.

  • 20 squats
  • 10 push-ups
  • 10 lunges each
  • 30 sec plank
  • Repeat 2-3 rounds

The EMOM

Every Minute On the Minute for 10 min.

  • Odd minutes: 10 burpees
  • Even minutes: 15 mountain climbers
  • Rest remainder of minute

Standard Workouts (20-25 min)

Full Body Circuit

40s work / 20s rest × 4 rounds

  • Squats
  • Push-ups
  • Reverse lunges
  • Mountain climbers
  • Plank

Tabata Stack

20s on / 10s off × 8 rounds each

  • Tabata 1: Squats
  • 1 min rest
  • Tabata 2: Push-ups
  • 1 min rest
  • Tabata 3: Burpees

Travel Tips

  • Time zone adjustment: Exercise helps reset your circadian rhythm—workout when you want to feel alert
  • Morning workouts: Get it done before the day gets away from you
  • Hotel gym: Even a basic treadmill or bike is useful for Zone 2 cardio
  • Pack light gear: Resistance bands and a jump rope fit anywhere
  • Stairs: Hotel stairwells = free cardio machine
