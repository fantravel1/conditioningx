8-Week Cardio Foundation

Build your aerobic base from scratch. Perfect for beginners.

8

Weeks

3-4x

Per Week

20-40

Min/Session

0

Equipment

Who This Program Is For

  • Complete beginners who haven't exercised in years (or ever)
  • People returning to fitness after illness, injury, or pregnancy
  • Anyone who gets winded walking up stairs
  • People who want to build a foundation before more intense training

Program Overview

This program builds your cardiovascular fitness gradually. We start with walking and progress to continuous light jogging by week 8. No rushingâ€”sustainable progress is the goal.

Week 1-2: Foundation

Goal: Establish the habit. Movement over intensity.

Week 1

  • Day 1: 15 min walk (comfortable pace)
  • Day 2: Rest
  • Day 3: 15 min walk
  • Day 4: Rest
  • Day 5: 15 min walk
  • Day 6-7: Rest or light activity

Week 2

  • Day 1: 18 min walk
  • Day 2: Rest
  • Day 3: 18 min walk
  • Day 4: Rest
  • Day 5: 20 min walk
  • Day 6-7: Rest or light activity

Week 3-4: Building

Goal: Increase duration. Add fourth day optional.

Week 3

  • Day 1: 20 min brisk walk
  • Day 2: 15 min gentle movement (yoga, stretching)
  • Day 3: 20 min brisk walk
  • Day 4: Rest
  • Day 5: 22 min brisk walk
  • Day 6-7: Optional: 15 min easy walk

Week 4

  • Day 1: 25 min brisk walk
  • Day 2: 15 min bodyweight moves
  • Day 3: 25 min brisk walk
  • Day 4: Rest
  • Day 5: 25 min brisk walk
  • Day 6-7: Optional: 20 min easy walk

Week 5-6: Walk-Run Introduction

Goal: Introduce running intervals. Walk-run method.

Week 5

  • Day 1: 25 min (walk 4 min, jog 1 min, repeat)
  • Day 2: 20 min easy walk
  • Day 3: 25 min walk-run (same pattern)
  • Day 4: Rest
  • Day 5: 25 min walk-run
  • Day 6-7: Optional: 15 min walk

Week 6

  • Day 1: 28 min (walk 3 min, jog 2 min, repeat)
  • Day 2: 20 min easy walk or cross-train
  • Day 3: 28 min walk-run
  • Day 4: Rest
  • Day 5: 30 min walk-run
  • Day 6-7: Optional activity

Week 7-8: Building Run Time

Goal: Increase running intervals. Build toward continuous running.

Week 7

  • Day 1: 30 min (walk 2 min, jog 3 min, repeat)
  • Day 2: Cross-training or rest
  • Day 3: 30 min walk-run
  • Day 4: Rest
  • Day 5: 32 min walk-run
  • Day 6-7: Optional: easy activity

Week 8

  • Day 1: 32 min (walk 1 min, jog 4 min, repeat)
  • Day 2: Cross-training or rest
  • Day 3: 35 min walk-run
  • Day 4: Rest
  • Day 5: 35 min (try 20 min continuous jog!)
