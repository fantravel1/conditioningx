Equipment

Before You Start

  • Properly sized jump rope (handles at armpit height when standing on rope)
  • Supportive shoes (cross-trainers or running shoes)
  • Hard, flat surface (avoid carpet)
  • Timer app or interval timer

The Workout

Format: 30 seconds jumping, 30 seconds rest. Different skills each round.

Warm Up (2 minutes)

  • 30 sec: Light bouncing in place (no rope)
  • 30 sec: Arm circles forward
  • 30 sec: Arm circles backward
  • 30 sec: Slow basic jumps (finding your rhythm)

  • 30 sec: Walk in place, shake out legs

Modifications

Easier

  • 20 sec work / 40 sec rest
  • Skip single leg rounds
  • Slow down the speed rounds
  • 8 rounds instead of 12

Harder

  • 45 sec work / 15 sec rest
  • Add double-unders
  • Add criss-cross rounds
  • 15-20 rounds total
