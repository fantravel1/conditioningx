Role at ConditioningX

Nutrition-Cardio Integration

Dr. Norton's expertise in nutritional science helps our users understand how to fuel their conditioning work. His research on protein timing, metabolic adaptation, and body composition informs our nutrition-adjacent content.

Metabolic Myth Buster

Layne is famous for debunking fitness myths with research. His evidence-based approach helps combat misinformation about cardio "killing gains" or metabolic damage from exercise.

Why He's Essential

Dr. Norton combines world-class athletic achievement with rigorous academic credentials. As both a PhD scientist and world record powerlifter, he embodies the principle that strength and conditioning are complementary, not competing priorities.
