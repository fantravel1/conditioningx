Calorie Burn Estimator

Estimate energy expenditure during cardiovascular activities.

About This Calculator

Calorie estimates are based on MET (Metabolic Equivalent of Task) values from the Compendium of Physical Activities. Individual results may vary based on:

  • Age and fitness level
  • Actual exercise intensity
  • Body composition
  • Environmental conditions

MET Values Reference

MET = energy cost compared to sitting quietly (1 MET)

Activity METs
Walking (3 mph) 3.5
Running (6 mph) 9.8
Cycling (12 mph) 8.0
Swimming (moderate) 7.0
Jump Rope 11.0
HIIT Training 12.0

A Note on Calories

At ConditioningX, we focus on health and capability, not calorie burning. Exercise provides countless benefits beyond energy expenditure: improved cardiovascular health, mental wellbeing, strength, and endurance. This tool is for informational purposes only.
