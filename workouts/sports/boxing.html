Bag Work Conditioning

Heavy Bag Intervals

Structure like a fight: 3-minute rounds, 1-minute rest.

  • Round 1-2: Technical work, moderate pace (warm up)
  • Round 3-4: Power shots, dig in
  • Round 5-6: Volume punching, constant output
  • Round 7-8: Combination flurries every 30 sec
  • Round 9-10: Last 30 sec of each round ALL OUT

This is your fight simulation. Make it real.

Sprint Work

Hill Sprints (Fighter Edition)

Find a steep hill, 30-50 meters long.

  • Sprint up all-out
  • Walk down (active recovery)
  • Start with 8 sprints, build to 15
  • 2x per week, not on sparring days

Builds explosive leg drive and mental toughness. When your legs go, your arms follow.
