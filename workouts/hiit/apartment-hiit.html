Before You Start

  • Clear a 6x6 ft space
  • Have water nearby
  • Set timer for work/rest intervals
  • Tell your roommates you'll be done in 20

The Workout

Format: 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest. 4 rounds of 5 exercises.

1 Speed Squats

40 seconds work | 20 seconds rest

  • Feet shoulder-width, toes slightly out
  • Squat down until thighs parallel to ground
  • Drive up quickly, squeeze glutes at top
  • Control the descentâ€”no stomping

Modification: Slow down the pace, add pause at bottom

2 Silent Burpees

40 seconds work | 20 seconds rest

  • Squat down, hands to floor
  • Step back one leg at a time to plank
  • Lower chest to floor
  • Push up, step feet forward, stand up
  • No jumpingâ€”step everything

Modification: Skip the pushup, just plank position

3 Mountain Climber Walk

40 seconds work | 20 seconds rest

  • High plank position
  • Step one foot forward toward hand
  • Step back, switch sides
  • Move as fast as you can while staying silent
  • Keep hips low, core tight

Modification: Slow the pace, focus on range of motion

4 Reverse Lunge Pulse

40 seconds work | 20 seconds rest

  • Step back into reverse lunge
  • Pulse 3 times at the bottom
  • Return to standing
  • Alternate legs each rep
  • Keep front knee over ankle

Modification: Skip the pulses, just do slow lunges

5 Plank to Pushup

40 seconds work | 20 seconds rest

  • Start in forearm plank
  • Push up to hand one arm at a time
  • Lower back to forearms one arm at a time
  • Alternate which arm leads
  • Keep hips as still as possible

Modification: Drop knees to floor

Repeat 4 Rounds

After completing all 5 exercises, rest 1 minute, then repeat. 4 total rounds = 20 minutes.
