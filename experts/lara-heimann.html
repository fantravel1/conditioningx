Role at ConditioningX

Mobility and Recovery Content

Cardiovascular conditioning creates demand on the body that requires quality recovery. Lara's physical therapy background ensures our mobility content is therapeutic, not just stretching.

Movement Quality Foundation

Before you run, cycle, or jump, you need to move well. Lara's LYT method provides the functional movement screening and correction our users need.

Why She's Essential

Most cardio injuries come from poor movement patterns compounded over thousands of repetitions. Lara's unique combination of physical therapy expertise and accessible yoga instruction helps prevent the injuries that derail conditioning progress.
