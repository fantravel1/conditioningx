Training Pace Zones

Based on your easy/conversational pace:

  • Easy/Recovery: 60-90 sec slower than race pace
  • Long Run: 30-60 sec slower than race pace
  • Tempo: 15-30 sec slower than race pace
  • Threshold: Current race pace
  • Intervals: 10-30 sec faster than race pace
