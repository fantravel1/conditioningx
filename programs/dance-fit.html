Why Dance Cardio?

Research shows people exercise longer and more consistently when they enjoy it. Dance cardio burns 300-600 calories per hour while improving coordination, mood, and cardiovascular health. Plus, it's fun.

Program Structure

Four weeks of progressive dance workouts. No experience neededâ€”just willingness to move and have fun.

Week 1-2: Foundation

Focus: Learning basic moves and rhythms

  • 3 sessions per week, 20-25 minutes
  • Simple step-touch patterns
  • Grapevines and slides
  • Basic arm movements
  • Slower tempo music (100-115 BPM)

Week 3-4: Intensity

Focus: Longer sessions, more complex choreography

  • 4 sessions per week, 30-40 minutes
  • Combination sequences
  • Higher intensity moves
  • Faster tempo music (120-140 BPM)
  • Add jumps and turns (optional)
