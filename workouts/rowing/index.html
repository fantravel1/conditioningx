Understanding Rowing Metrics

Split Time

X:XX/500m

Time to row 500 meters. Lower is faster. Sub-2:00 is solid.

Stroke Rate (SPM)

18-32 spm

Strokes per minute. Higher isn't always better - power matters.

Watts

Power Output

True power measurement. 200W+ is strong recreational rowing.
