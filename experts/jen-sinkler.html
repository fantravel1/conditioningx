Philosophy: "Lift Weights Faster"

When asked what she does for cardio, Jen famously replied: "I lift weights faster." This encapsulates her approach‚Äîusing strength training with strategic rest periods and movement selection to build cardiovascular fitness without traditional cardio.

The LWF Method

  • Metabolic Resistance Training - Strength work that elevates heart rate
  • Density Training - More work in less time
  • Complexes - Multiple exercises without putting weight down
  • Finishers - Short, intense work after strength training
  • Varied Rest Periods - Strategic recovery for different effects

Sample Conditioning Formats

Kettlebell Complex (12 min)

Set timer for 12 minutes. Complete as many rounds as possible:

  • 5 Kettlebell swings
  • 5 Goblet squats
  • 5 KB deadlifts
  • 5 KB presses (each side)

Rest as needed. Focus on quality movement at speed.

Density Ladder (15 min)

Set timer for 15 minutes. Complete:

  • 10 Pushups, 10 Squats
  • 9 Pushups, 9 Squats
  • 8, 8... down to 1, 1

Beat your time next session.

Every Minute on the Minute (10 min)

At the start of each minute:

  • Odd minutes: 10 KB swings
  • Even minutes: 5 burpees

Whatever time remains is your rest.

Why This Works for Conditioning

Physiological Benefits

  • Elevated heart rate throughout
  • EPOC (afterburn effect)
  • Builds muscle while conditioning
  • Improves work capacity

Practical Benefits

  • Time efficient (15-20 min)
  • Never boring
  • Measurable progress
  • Minimal equipment needed
