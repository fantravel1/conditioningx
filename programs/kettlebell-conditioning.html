Kettlebell Conditioning

Kettlebell

The Pavel Approach

Inspired by Pavel Tsatsouline's "Simple & Sinister" methodology. Two movements—swings and Turkish get-ups—deliver comprehensive conditioning. This program adds variety while maintaining the minimalist philosophy.

Equipment Needed

Women: Start with 8-12kg (18-26 lbs)

Men: Start with 16-24kg (35-53 lbs)

One bell is enough. You'll progress through volume before adding weight.

Core Movements

Kettlebell Swing

The foundation. Develops hip power, posterior chain strength, and cardiovascular conditioning simultaneously.

Turkish Get-Up

Full-body coordination and stability. Builds strength from the ground up.

Week 1-2: Foundation

3 sessions per week:

  • Swings: 5 sets of 10 (50 total)
  • Get-Ups: 1 per side, 5 times (10 total)
  • Rest as needed between sets

Week 3-4: Building

4 sessions per week:

  • Swings: 10 sets of 10 (100 total)
  • Get-Ups: 5 per side (10 total)
  • Add goblet squats: 3 sets of 5

Week 5-6: Conditioning

4 sessions per week:

  • Swings: Work toward 100 in under 5 minutes
  • Get-Ups: 10 total, alternating
  • Add: Cleans, presses, or snatches
