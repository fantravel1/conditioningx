Tennis Fitness Demands

Tennis is a game of repeated short explosions. Each point lasts just seconds, but matches can go for hours. You need explosive lateral movement, a powerful first step, and the aerobic base to recover between points while maintaining technique under fatigue.

Court Movement Drills

15 min

Spider Drill

Court coverageClassic
  • Start at center baseline
  • Touch all 5 points (corners + net center)
  • Return to center each time
  • Time yourself. Beat it next time.
12 min

Lateral Shuffle Series

Side-to-sideFoundation
  • Alley to alley shuffles x 10
  • Crossover steps x 10
  • Split step + shuffle x 10
  • 2 sets, 90 sec rest between

First Step Explosiveness

Reaction Sprint Drill

Partner calls direction (left/right/forward/back) - you react and sprint 3-5 steps.

  • 20 reactions per set
  • Full recovery between reps
  • 3 sets total
  • Focus: explosive first step, staying low

The first step wins or loses the point. Train it specifically.
