The Hybrid Approach

Contrary to popular belief, you CAN build strength and cardiovascular fitness simultaneously. The key is strategic programming. This plan combines strength training days with cardio days in a way that maximizes both adaptations.

Weekly Structure

  • Day 1: Lower body strength
  • Day 2: Cardio - Zone 2 (30-40 min)
  • Day 3: Upper body strength
  • Day 4: Rest or active recovery
  • Day 5: Full body strength
  • Day 6: HIIT or tempo cardio (20-30 min)
  • Day 7: Rest

Strength Days

Focus on compound movements with moderate volume. Not bodybuilding, but functional strength.

Lower Body

  • Squats
  • Deadlifts or RDLs
  • Lunges
  • Glute bridges

Upper Body

  • Push-ups or bench
  • Rows
  • Overhead press
  • Pull-ups or lat pulldown

Cardio Days

Zone 2 Days: Easy effort you can maintain while talking. Running, cycling, rowing, or swimming.

HIIT Days: Short intervals (20-30 sec) with recovery. Builds power and VO2 max.
