Apartment Cardio

Neighbor-friendly, low-impact cardio. No jumping, no noise complaints.

4

Weeks

4x

Per Week

20-30

Minutes

0

Equipment

Perfect For

Anyone living in an apartment, shared housing, or needing quiet workouts. All movements are low-impact—no jumping or stomping. Your downstairs neighbors will thank you.

How It Works

Four workouts per week using only low-impact movements. We build intensity through speed and time-under-tension rather than jumping.

Movement Library

Lower Body

  • Squats (slow tempo)
  • Reverse lunges
  • Curtsy lunges
  • Step touches
  • Standing leg lifts

Upper Body + Core

  • Push-ups
  • Mountain climbers (slow)
  • Planks
  • Bird dogs
  • Dead bugs

Cardio Moves

  • High knees (marching)
  • Fast feet (quiet)
  • Lateral shuffles
  • Standing oblique crunches
  • Arm circles at pace

Weekly Structure

  • Day 1: Lower body focus - 25 min
  • Day 2: Rest or walking
  • Day 3: Full body LIIT (Low-Impact Interval Training) - 20 min
  • Day 4: Rest
  • Day 5: Core + cardio - 25 min
  • Day 6: Full body strength-cardio - 30 min
  • Day 7: Rest

Sample Workout: LIIT Circuit

Format: 40 sec work / 20 sec rest × 4 rounds

  1. Slow mountain climbers
  2. Squat to calf raise
  3. Push-up to shoulder tap
  4. Reverse lunge (alternating)
  5. Plank
