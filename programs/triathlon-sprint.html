Sprint Triathlon Training

Swim. Bike. Run. Your entry into the world of multi-sport endurance.

12

Weeks

5-6x

Per Week

4-8

Hrs/Week

3

Sports

Sprint Distance

Swim: 750m (0.47 miles) | Bike: 20km (12.4 miles) | Run: 5km (3.1 miles)

The sprint distance is perfect for beginners. Challenging but achievable with consistent training.

Weekly Structure

  • Day 1: Swim
  • Day 2: Bike
  • Day 3: Run
  • Day 4: Rest or easy swim
  • Day 5: Bike + Run (brick workout)
  • Day 6: Long workout (rotating discipline)
  • Day 7: Rest

Weeks 1-4: Foundation

Focus: Build competency in each discipline

  • Swim: 2x/week, 20-30 min. Focus on technique over distance.
  • Bike: 2x/week, 30-45 min. Get comfortable in the saddle.
  • Run: 2x/week, 20-30 min. Easy conversational pace.

Weeks 5-8: Building

Focus: Increase volume, introduce brick workouts

  • Add 10-15% weekly volume
  • Start bike-to-run brick workouts (practice transitions)
  • One longer session per week in each discipline

Weeks 9-11: Race Prep

Focus: Race simulation, confidence building

  • Practice open water swimming if race is outdoors
  • Full distance practice in each sport (not all same day)
  • Practice transitions (T1 and T2)

Week 12: Taper

Focus: Rest, recover, race ready

  • Reduce volume by 40-50%
  • Keep some intensity but shorter sessions
  • Focus on sleep, nutrition, logistics
