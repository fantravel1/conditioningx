Soccer Fitness Demands

Soccer requires an aerobic base for 90 minutes of movement, plus anaerobic power for sprints, jumps, and tackles. You need to recover between high-intensity efforts while maintaining technique under fatigue. This is why soccer players are among the fittest athletes in any sport.

Off-Season Development

Build What's Weak

Off-season is for developing qualities you can't build in-season. If you fade late in games, build aerobic base. If you can't beat players, build sprint speed.

Cross-Training

Swimming, cycling, and rowing build fitness without the impact of running. Give your legs a break while maintaining conditioning.
